Tim Bergling, known professionally as Avicii, was a Swedish DJ, remixer, and record producer. His musical style is primarily House music, and he is an influence on many artists. Several music publications have credited Avicii as one of the DJs who took electronic music to Top 40 radio in the early 2010s. He is also considered one of the most popular and successful Electronic dance music (EDM) genre artists of all time.
